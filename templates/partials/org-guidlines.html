Organization Registration Information

Thank you for considering registering your organization for blood donation! Below are the essential details for organization registration:

  • Provide accurate and complete information about your organization
  • Include the name of the organization, the head of the organization, and contact details
  • Upload the organization logo for identification purposes
  • Provide a valid email address for communication
  • Include the organization's physical address
  • Ensure the head of the organization is reachable via the provided phone number
  • Organization profile will be visible upon successful registration
  • For any queries or clarification regarding the registration process, feel free to contact us.

We appreciate your commitment to supporting blood donation initiatives. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.
