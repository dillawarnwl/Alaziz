FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

How do I log in to my account?

To Login, you need to first sign up. After signing up, you will receive an email containing a verification link. Click on the link to verify your email, and then you can log in using your credentials.

How do I register as a blood donor?

To register as a blood donor, click on the "Register as Donor" button. Complete the form, ensuring to fill in the box related to your last donation accurately. If you haven't donated blood recently, please provide a date at least 90 days earlier, as profiles with donations within the last 90 days will not be displayed.

Why is it important to accurately enter the last donation date?

Entering your last donation date accurately is crucial for maintaining transparency and ensuring that potential recipients have the most reliable information about donor eligibility. Profiles with donations within the last 90 days may not be displayed, so providing the correct date is essential for an effective blood donation system.

What information should I update over time?

It's essential to keep your current city information up-to-date for a better user experience. Additionally, regularly update your last donation date to provide accurate information about your donation history.

How can I update my information?

Log in to your account, go to the profile section, and click on the Edit icon button. From there, you can update your current city and last donation date.

How to Find a Donor?

To find a donor on our website, search by city and blood group, click on the chosen donor's profile picture, and use the Contact Me button on their profile page for direct communication without intermediaries.
